About Us

Barber Bros isn’t just a barbershop—it’s an experience. We’re redefining what it means to visit a barbershop by creating a space where style, fun, and relaxation meet.

Enjoy a bar for parents, a play corner for kids, and activities that keep you entertained while you wait. From laptops and iPads to a gaming screen for teens, there’s something for everyone.

We also offer a private section where children can enjoy their haircut while parents relax with a drink. Saturdays are extra special with a free drink between 4 and 5 PM!

Prices are clearly displayed, and we even offer haircut packages for a complete experience. Want more? Try our special activities—because at Barber Bros, it’s more than just a haircut.
